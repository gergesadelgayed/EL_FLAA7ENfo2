The Birth of EL FLA7EN

EL FLA7EN was forged at the Faculty of Computers & Information, Luxor University, Egypt. Six cyber security enthusiasts — united by ambition and driven by a shared desire to master the digital battlefield — built this unit from scratch.

The name "FLA7EN" — meaning "farmers" in Arabic — is a declaration: we cultivate knowledge, harvest skills, and plant the seeds of a stronger security culture in Egypt. We do not merely solve CTF challenges. We build operators.
